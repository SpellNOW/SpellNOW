About Us

A small business with big ambitions.

We are a small business that wanted to revolutionize literacy education, specifically in spelling and vocabulary. After consulting with numerous educators and parents regarding what they want from literacy software, we designed a system which will allow students to practice key concept areas in literacy:

  • Spelling
  • Vocabulary
  • Roots

SpellNOW!™ started out as a project that would allow my sister to excel in spelling bees using an approach that differed from today's spelling software. After much trial and error, we have created a software that will be able to support students, teachers, and parents, in literacy education. - CEO and Founder, Lakshya Chauhan
