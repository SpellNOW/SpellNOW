Hello!

Note: All pill icons on this page are linked to external pages and are clickable.

I'm Lakshya and I'm a high school student based in New Jersey. I'm a highly motivated individual with a strong background in technology, leadership, and community service.

Since 2021 I've been working as Webmaster for SpellNOW! maintaining our current library and adding to its features. If you're a SpellNOW! user, thank you so much for your support! It's thanks to users like you that I keep the website running! I'm always looking for ways to improve the platform, so if you want to share your thoughts, please feel free to reach out to me at support@spellnow.org. I'm always open to feedback and suggestions!

I am also a Research Intern at the New Jersey Institute of Technology and have worked with Dr. Akshay Rangamani to decode neural circuits within RNNs.

I am also actively involved in the Civil Air Patrol and am an instructor for their STEM program. I have previously served as a volunteer tutor for programs such as the North South Foundation and schoolhouse.world. I also have participated in the CyberPatriot and USACO series of competitions.

When I'm not working, I enjoy playing the piano, reading, and playing tennis; in fact I am a huge fan of Jefferey Archer novels.

You can view more of my projects on GitHub. I also have old music videos available on my YouTube channel.
